# Skill Quality Gate

`ome evolve` verifies generated skill candidates before adoption. The gate prevents vague or unverifiable skills from becoming durable project guidance.

Required sections:

- Purpose
- When to Use
- Inputs
- Process
- Red Flags
- Common Rationalizations
- Verification
- Output Contract

Scoring dimensions:

- `specificity`
- `actionability`
- `verification`
- `scope_control`
- `reuse_of_existing_patterns`
- `risk_awareness`

Default adoption thresholds:

- total score must be at least 24 out of 30
- `verification` must be at least 4
- `scope_control` must be at least 4

Reject candidates that encourage skipping tests, skipping verification, broad unrelated cleanup, unrequested dependencies, or copying third-party guidance verbatim.
